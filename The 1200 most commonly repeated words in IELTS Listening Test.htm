    The 1200 most commonly repeated words in IELTS Listening Test


    Most students consider the listening and speaking the easiest parts of the IELTS test. As we know that
    Spelling words is difficult to many of us even native speakers of English have problems with spelling. For this reason I posted this topic and I hope you will find these information useful


    First, in the listening section of the IELTS Exam, if you spell a word incorrectly then it is wrong. This is a list of the 1200 most commonly repeated words that you should know how to spell in order to avoid losing invaluable marks
    Click here to download the file

    The second thing, I would like to mention is that there are may ways to improve your spelling skill. For example, to reduce vowel mistake you could create your own sentences that have particular vowel letter patterns
    Ex: words that contain au
    .My aunt's daughter ate dinosaur meat the restaurant
    .Identity fraud and Money laundering are faults
    .Astronauts launched from Australia
    Even though these sentences are not logical, they serve their purpose of creation

    The third point, knowing root words can help you to spell them correctly
    :Ex
    hydro (Greek) water = hydropower, hydroelectricity¡ hydrogen
    astr or aster (Greek) star = asteroid, astronomy, astronaut
    aqua (Latin) water = aquarium
    herb (Latin) grass = herbivore¡ herbicide
    vore (Latin) eater = carnivore, herbivore¡ omnivore
    terr (Latin) earth = territory
    ped (Latin) foot = pedestrian, pedal
    scrib, script (Latin) to write = describe, script, subscription, inscribe
    cent (Latin) hundred = percent, centigrade
    In German. "Kinder" = children and “Garten” = garden == kindergarten

    Also understanding how to form nouns from verbs by adding the suffix and prefix is helpful
    subscribe = subscription
    describe = description
    inscribe = inscription
    http://ekmekci.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/Appendix-C.pdf

    Finally, sometimes you find some works are difficult to spell, so in this situation if you separate them in particular way, they will be essay to memorize their spelling
    :Ex
    ant act ice = Antarctica
    per man ent = permanent
    temper ature = temperature
    amer I can = american
